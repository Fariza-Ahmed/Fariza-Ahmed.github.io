3D Kitchen Model

by Fariza Ahmed

Description

I designed a scene that depicts an entryway to a room with a kitchen. Towards the viewer’s right, there is a kitchen with two counters each with cabinets above. The counters and cabinets have a wooden texture and the countertops have a marble texture. There is also a sink included in the kitchen. The highlight of the kitchen is the tiles which are placed between the bottom counters and top cabinets. The tiles were created using separate square, flat surfaces with the texture that is seen. The counters and cabinets were created using cylinders and separate flat surfaces. Cylinders were also used for the sink and again a flat surface for the texture of the inside of the sink. Straight ahead in the viewers direction is a door texture.

To the left of the viewpoint is a table with two stool chairs. Both the table and chairs were created with cylinders. The tops of the chairs have a straw texture. The wall directly behind the table and chairs has an artsy texture and the wall to the left of the table and chairs has a texture of a screen door with blinds showing a mock view of the scene outside the room. The entire floor of the room is a light wood texture. There are three light sources in the scene. One placed more towards the viewer’s right which is intended to be some sort of light source in the apartment. The other two are placed to the viewer’s left and are intended to mock light coming into the room from outside. One light represents day and the other represents night. All of the colors, textures, and lights were chosen to compliment the theme of the scene.

Features

  • Pressing the arrow keys changes the view position.
  • Pressing “HOME” and “END” changes the distance of the scene from the viewer.
  • Pressing “w” key toggles wire frame mode.
  • Pressing “c” toggles culling back faces.
  • The program always uses Phong interpolation.
  • Pressing “1”, “2”, and “3” toggles the three lights off and on.
  • Pressing e, a, d, s toggles the rendering of emissive, ambient, diffuse, and specular light.
  • Press the escape key to exit.
